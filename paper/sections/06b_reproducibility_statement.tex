\section*{Reproducibility Statement}

The anonymized supplementary repository includes the benchmark
instances, generator, evaluation harness, prompts, analysis scripts,
exact dependency metadata, and saved result artifacts needed to inspect
the benchmark design and evaluation protocol.
It includes
\texttt{pyproject.toml} and \texttt{uv.lock}, deterministic data
generation code, stratified split definitions, and the derived JSON
artifacts used for leaderboard summaries, significance tests, and deeper
error analysis.
The manuscript sources and rendered figures are maintained separately
from the review supplement.
All main-text comparative claims are restricted to matched evaluation
pools, and the paper explicitly separates the Large-500 and
Controlled-100 settings to avoid unsupported cross-pool comparisons.
