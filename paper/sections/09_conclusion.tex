\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

\benchmark{} reveals an aggregation gap in four of six frontier LLMs: balance sheets replayed from the models' own entries are substantially more accurate than the ones they report. A rule-based forced ledger verifier closes this gap by $+25$ to $+37$\,pp \bsexact{} across four model families with no fine-tuning, at a documented inconsistency-detection cost on smaller models. Capability is present in the underlying models; self-consistency is the bottleneck.
